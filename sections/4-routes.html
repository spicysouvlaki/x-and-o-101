Reading Routes

The QB picks a route based on what the defense shows. Here are the 6 routes you'll see most often.

Slant

When to use: Defense is in man coverage

Quick inside break at 45 degrees, beats man coverage.

✓ Reward Quick completion, hard to defend
✗ Risk Linebacker can jump it for interception

Watch for:

  • QB throwing quickly after the snap
  • Receiver breaking sharply inside
