Reading the Defense

As a QB, you need to recognize what the defense is doing before the snap.

Play:

Showing typical coverage assignments. The same look can disguise different plays (blitz, zone, or man).

Two-High Shell (Cover 2/4)

Two safeties deep split the field in half. Corners play underneath. Vulnerable to deep middle and seam routes.

Effectiveness:

vs Deep Pass
vs Short Pass
vs Run

Weak Against:

  • Deep middle (hole shot)
  • Seam routes
  • Corner routes

Best Used: Base defense, safe look

QB Tips to Beat It:

  • Look for the seam between safeties
  • Attack the corners with deep outs
  • Use the middle of the field
