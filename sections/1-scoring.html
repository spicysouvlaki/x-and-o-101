X's and O's 101

Learn to watch football like you actually know what's going on.

What is Football?

Two teams of 11 players take turns on offense (trying to score) and defense (trying to stop them). The offense moves the ball down the field toward the opponent's end zone.

How Does It Work?

The offense gets 4 attempts (called downs) to move the ball 10 yards. Make it 10 yards? You get 4 more tries. Fail? The other team gets the ball.

Most plays are either a run (hand the ball to a runner) or a pass (throw it to a receiver). The defense tries to stop both.

How Scoring Works

The goal is simple: get the ball into the end zone to score.
