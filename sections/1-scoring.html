How Scoring Works

Football is simple: move the ball into the end zone to score.

6 Touchdown Ball crosses goal line
+1/2 Extra Point Kick (+1) or 2-pt conversion (+2)
3 Field Goal Kick through uprights

So to score a touchdown, all you have to do is throw the ball into the end zone. Super easy, right?
